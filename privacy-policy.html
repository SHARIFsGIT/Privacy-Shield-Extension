🛡️ Privacy Policy - Privacy Shield Pro

Last Updated: December 2024

Effective Date: December 2024

📋 Overview

Privacy Shield Pro is a Chrome extension designed to protect your sensitive information on web pages. This privacy policy explains how our extension handles your information and protects your privacy.

🔒 Key Point: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

🚫 Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data whatsoever.

  • ✅ All processing happens locally on your device
  • ✅ No personal information is collected or stored
  • ✅ No data is sent to external servers
  • ✅ No analytics, tracking, or telemetry
  • ✅ No user behavior monitoring

🔧 Permissions Usage

Our extension requests specific permissions solely for functionality:

  • activeTab: To apply privacy protection to the current webpage
  • storage: To save your preferences locally on your device only
  • notifications: To display status updates about protection actions
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click menu options
  • desktopCapture: To enable screenshot functionality with privacy protection
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>): To work on all websites where you need privacy protection

💾 Local Storage

The extension stores only your preference settings locally:

  • Protection mode selection (blur, mask, or hide)
  • Blur intensity settings
  • Mask symbol preferences
  • Theme preferences (light/dark)
  • No personal or sensitive information is stored

🤝 Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

  • ❌ No data collection means no data sharing
  • ❌ No third-party integrations
  • ❌ No external analytics services
  • ❌ No advertising networks

🔐 Security

All extension functionality operates entirely within your browser:

  • ✅ No external network connections
  • ✅ No data transmission to remote servers
  • ✅ All privacy protection happens locally
  • ✅ Your data never leaves your device

👶 Children's Privacy

Our extension does not collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

🌍 International Users

Since we don't collect any data, GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations are inherently complied with.

📝 Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

✅ Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
